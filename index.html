SETTING THE BENCHMARK FOR EXCELLENCE IN BEAUTY & WELLNESS

Myrah is the luxury beauty & wellness destination in Mumbai. With over a decade of experience, and a commitment to providing exceptional service and a serene environment, we offer a truly indulgent and rejuvenating experience for our guests.

From our luxurious facilities, our attention to detail in every aspect of the wellness experience, and our warm and welcoming hospitality, we strive to create an oasis where our guests can escape from the stress of everyday life and feel happy.

NISHA JAVERI

Founder & CEO

Keenly aware of the transformative influence that beauty and wellness could have, Nisha has always been disciplined about her health. However, she noticed that there was a distinct lack of quality offerings in the industry. Driven by a desire to elevate the standards of luxury, she decided to start Myrah in 2007. A spa that at its core embodied the very essence of quality and luxury. Through consistency, discipline, and unmatched attention to detail, Myrah has grown into an renowned brand, known for its unwavering commitment to excellence. Myrah has since expanded into salon and dermatology services, providing guests with a complete and unparalleled luxury experience that caters to all of their needs.For this reason Nisha ensured that Myrah only hired the most qualified dermatologists and beauty professions, and utilised the highest quality equipment, ensuring that guests receive the highest quality of care and exceptional results.Throughout it all, Nisha’s refusal to compromise on quality, even when faced with numerous cheaper alternatives, has cemented Myrah’s position as a leader in the industry; where the transformative power of beauty and wellness paired with luxury and excellence offer guests an unmatched experience.
